Quantum mechanics is a branch of physics dealing with motion and interaction of subat omic particles. This incorporates the concepts of energy such as wave/particle duality, as well as the uncertainty principle, represented by the schrodinger's cat paradox where the cat is simultaneously dead and alive at the same time. This also includes the multiple dimensions and universes theory, called the many-worlds theory, where every single decision you make branches off into infinite amounts o f dimensions based on which decision you chose. It keeps splitting off as decisions are made. It is th e theory of nature at very very small, subatomic levels, and the inherent randomness in the world.
