Wormholes

Wormholes are a solution to the field equations made by Einstein in relation to quantum mechanics, which is linked here: Wormholes are essentially portals to another position in spacetime, bending it so that links can be formed like when folding over a piece of paper. For example, point A is in the top left and point B in the bottom right of an A4 page. They are far apart, but when you fold the paper they can be touching. This is essentially the logic that wormholes follow, creating a path between points in spacetime and sometimes even dimensions.
